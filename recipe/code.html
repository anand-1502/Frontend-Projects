HOW TO MAKE CHICKEN BIRYANI

I love chicken biryani as it is a complete meal itself. It has all the flavours blended perfectly into each other. The chicken is tender and juicy to give the perfect taste and the rice accompanies it by having spices in it. This results in a perfect combination. Which is not too heavy or too light. It is even so beautiful to see a perfectly made chicken biryani with hot steam coming out of it.

Ingredients required

  • 250g chicken
    • boneless or with bone as per liking
  • Basmati rice
    • should have AGED mark
  • whole spices
  • biryani masala/garam masala
  • ghee
